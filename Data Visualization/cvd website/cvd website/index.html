Analysis of the risk factors causing heart disease

This webapp shows the overall analysis of the risk factors causing heart disease. And to create an awareness among people about the social and biological risk factors that cause cardiovascular disease.

We can use computer aided machines to improve the medical diagnosis which is be developed using the Python programming and visulized using Tableau displayed through storyboard.

BIOLOGICAL FACTORS



Intial analysis was made on the basis of the various biological factors causing cardiovascular heart disease. Biological factors such as Blood Pressure, Maximum Heart Rate, Cholesterol Level with respect to Gender, Age and the Presence and Absence of Heart Disease. SOCIAL FACTORS



Like Biological Factors , Social Factors like Marital status, Smoking, HyperTension, Residence, Work Place and Type could also be a major risk factor causing cardiovascular heart disease. So both Exploratory Data Analysis and Machine Learing prediction was done to find the major factor.
